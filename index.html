About Me

The Quest to Gamify Reality

My journey began with a huge dream: to not just play in virtual worlds, but to make the real world itself a game. From founding my first studio in Indonesia to leading XR innovation in Japan, every line of code I write paves the path toward that reality.

I specialize in Unity and Clean Architecture. I believe that to build a complex reality, one needs a solid foundation. That's why I dedicate myself to open-source tools like Kassets and SOAR, helping other developers build scalable systems.

Currently, I'm pushing the boundaries of what's possible by integrating Generative AI into game loops and exploring the frontiers of Spatial Computing.
